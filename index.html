Everything you would love to know

Haven't found the answer here? Contact us.

How can I organize a concert?

Once we've launched, you'll be able to create a nice concert page here at RASSEYE!. Share the page with your town and let them vote for your concert! View detailed voting statistics and browse similar concerts with our freemium tools. If your voting succeeds, we will publish the results so that your star could hear and listen to you.

Do I have to pre-buy a ticket when I'm voting for some concert?

No! We don't organize crowdfunding. We will contact you when (and if) the voting succeeds.

Do you guarantee me the concert if my voting succeeds?

Unfortunately, we can't guarantee that the concert will take place. Our main idea is to show the artists how many people want them in different places. We emphasize on collected statistics. We publish detailed results. We believe that such actions will trigger new amazing concerts. Please note that we don't give you tickets if your concert is finally arranged as well.

Is it free?

Yes. Our tools are freemium. You pay only if you want to use advanced features.

Can I get back my donation?

No. Donations are not refundable. All money will be spent on RASSEYE! further development.

Can I donate in USD/EUR/CAD etc.?

At the moment, we accept BTC donations only. If you want to make a donation in other currency, contact us directly.
